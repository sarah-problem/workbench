Discrimination means being unfairly treated because of an individual characteristic, such as race or ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, social class, age, or disability.

Clinical Note

The TSDS measures the frequency of anxiety-related trauma symptoms attributed to discrimination. It does not determine whether an event meets DSM trauma criteria and does not independently establish PTSD or another diagnosis.
