LPFS-SR

Level of Personality Functioning Scale - Self Report

An 80-item self-report measure for adults age 18 and older. Rate how well each statement describes the client generally.

The published weighted scoring produces Identity, Self-Direction, Empathy, Intimacy, and Total scores. Some positively worded items have negative weights; they are not reverse-coded.

Results

Total weighted score
Interpret as a severity reference, not a diagnosis. The published markers are based on 306 non-clinical respondents. Scores above +1.0 SD may reflect subclinical problems; scores above +1.5 to +2.0 SD may indicate clinically significant dysfunction meriting further assessment, treatment, and follow-up. Clinical judgment should guide interpretation.
