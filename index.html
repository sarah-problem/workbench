Clinical Workbench

Assessment, screening, teaching, and documentation tools.

Assessment

Mental Status Exam

Document findings, review definitions, and generate narrative or list output.

Quick Risk Assessment

Document current risk factors, protective factors, clinical formulation, and interventions.

Comprehensive Suicide Assessment

Complete a fuller suicide inquiry with ideation, behavior, intensity, means, formulation, and recommendation.

Screening

PHQ-9

Screen depressive symptoms and generate documentation. For ages 11–17, use the one-week adolescent timeframe.

GAD-7

Screen anxiety symptoms and generate documentation, with age guidance for adolescent use.

ISAS

Document self-injury behaviors, score the 13 function scales, review results, and generate documentation.

MDQ

Screen for bipolar-spectrum symptoms, co-occurrence, and functional impairment.

ASRS v1.1

Complete the six-question adult ADHD screener and review shaded-response scoring.

PC-PTSD-5

Complete a brief trauma exposure and probable PTSD screen.

CRAFFT 2.1

Screen substance use and riding or driving risk for ages 12–21.

ACE Questionnaire

Document adverse childhood experience categories using trauma-informed guidance.

Positive Childhood Experiences

Identify childhood relational, family, school, and community strengths.

WHO-5

Measure current positive mental well-being over the past two weeks.

Y-BOCS / CY-BOCS

Document OCD symptom patterns and rate adult or child/adolescent severity in one guided tool.
