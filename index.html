Clinical Workbench

Assessment, screening, teaching, and documentation tools.

Assessment

Mental Status Exam

Record the exam and create a narrative or list-style note.

Quick Risk Assessment

Review current risk, protective factors, and the immediate plan.

Comprehensive Suicide Assessment

Complete an in-depth suicide assessment and clinical formulation.

Screening

PHQ-9

Brief depression screening for adults and adolescents.

GAD-7

Brief screening for generalized anxiety symptoms.

ISAS

Identify self-injury behaviors and the functions they serve.

MDQ

Screen for possible bipolar-spectrum symptoms.

ASRS v1.1

Six-item adult ADHD screening.

PC-PTSD-5

Brief adult PTSD screening.

CRAFFT 2.1

Substance-use screening for ages 12–21.

Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs)

Review exposure to common childhood adversities.

Benevolent Childhood Experiences (BCEs)

Review positive and supportive childhood experiences.

WHO-5

Brief measure of well-being over the past two weeks.

Y-BOCS/CY-BOCS

Rate OCD symptom severity in adults or youth.

Trauma Symptoms of Discrimination Scale (TSDS)

Measure trauma symptoms related to discrimination.

Level of Personality Functioning Scale (LPFS-SR)

Measure adult self and interpersonal functioning.

Functional Assessment of Self-Mutilation (FASM)

Review self-harm methods, frequency, and functions.

RCADS-47

Youth anxiety and depression screening.

DERS

Measure difficulties with emotion regulation.

CAT-Q

Measure autistic camouflaging in adults.

CATS

PTSD screening for ages 7–17.

PCL-5

Measure adult PTSD symptoms over the past month.
